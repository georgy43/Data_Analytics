# EDA on Retail Sales Data
## Objective
Perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a retail sales dataset to uncover patterns, trends, and actionable business insights.

---

## Dataset
- **Records:** 1,000 transactions
- **Features:** Transaction ID, Date, Customer ID, Gender, Age, Product Category, Quantity, Price per Unit, Total Amount

---

## Tools & Libraries
| Tool | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Python 3 | Core language |
| Pandas | Data manipulation |
| NumPy | Numerical operations |
| Matplotlib | Visualizations |
| Seaborn | Statistical plots |
| Google Colab | Development environment |

---

## Key Steps
1. **Data Loading & Cleaning** — Checked nulls, duplicates, fixed data types
2. **Feature Engineering** — Extracted Month, Day of Week, Quarter from Date
3. **Descriptive Statistics** — Mean, median, mode, std deviation on key columns
4. **Time Series Analysis** — Monthly revenue trends, best-performing days
5. **Customer Analysis** — Gender-wise and age-group-wise spending patterns
6. **Product Analysis** — Revenue breakdown by product category
7. **Correlation Heatmap** — Relationships between numerical variables
8. **Recommendations** — Actionable business insights from findings

---

## Key Insights
- Electronics, Clothing, and Beauty are the three product categories
- Revenue trends show clear seasonal patterns across months
- Customer age groups and gender show distinct purchasing behaviors
- Strong correlation between Price per Unit and Total Amount
